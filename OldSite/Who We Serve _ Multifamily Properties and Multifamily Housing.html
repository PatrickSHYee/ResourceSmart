Affordable Multifamily Housing

Low-income or fixed-income residents of affordable housing properties spend over four times the national average on their utility and health care costs. ResourceSmart’s green rehab is ideal for improving both of these issues, leading to lowered utility bills and healthier environments. We believe affordability is not just about rent and with our energy efficient upgrades we can address some of the crucial costs that hamper residents. Owners reap key benefits from the retrofits we provide including reduced utility costs and lowered O&M costs. Our programs are designed to benefit all parties, let us help you get started with your green rehab project today.
 

Senior & Disabled Housing Facilities

Senior and disabled housing facilities are often underserved group in energy efficient technology. Making green improvements to these types of facilities benefit the owner and the residents in key ways. The residents benefit from safer, healthier and more comfortable environments. These types of benefits are achieved through sealing drafts and hot/cold zones, increasing air quality, making more operable safer windows, improving lighting and lowering any utility costs. The owners benefit from reduced operating costs from lower utility and maintenance costs, increased property value and increased occupancy from the superior conditions.
